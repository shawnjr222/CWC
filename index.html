In a system that commodifies labor to generate profit, exploitation of that labor is inevitable. Mutual care is how we begin to heal the collective and individual harm caused by the systemic oppression found within capitalism.

Coping with capitalism is a publication that provides clear & accessible paths to self and collective care. By embracing and promoting a culture of mutual care, we will co-create a more inclusive & supportive world for all.
